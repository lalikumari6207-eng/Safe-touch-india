Our Journey

Our Story

From a bold idea to a trusted technology company — here's how Lyzonix came to be.

2021

The Beginning

Lyzonix Technologies was born from a simple belief: every Indian business deserves powerful digital tools without the premium price tag. We started with a small team, big dreams, and relentless passion for technology.

2022

First 50 Projects

We delivered our first 50 client projects — spanning websites, logos, and IT setups. Each project taught us something new and strengthened our resolve to deliver excellence consistently.

2023

Expanding Services

We expanded into mobile app development, SEO optimization, and advanced data analytics — becoming a full-spectrum digital agency. Our client base grew to 50+ businesses across India.

2024

1000+ Logos & Growing

We crossed the milestone of 1000+ logo designs and 100+ completed projects. Today, Lyzonix is one of India's most trusted boutique tech agencies, known for quality, speed, and genuine client care.

Future

What's Next

We are expanding our team, launching new AI-powered services, and building partnerships to help more Indian businesses go digital. The best of Lyzonix is still ahead.
